# Article 90: RESOLUTION / COMPREHENSIVE PLAN ENDORSEMENT

**Sponsor:** Inserted at the request of the Department of Planning and Community Development

## Description

To see if the Town will accept, receive, or resolve to endorse the Comprehensive Plan to be adopted by the Arlington Redevelopment Board; or take any action related thereto.
