# Article 14: BYLAW AMENDMENT / TREE COMMITTEE RULES AND PROCEDURES

**Sponsor:** Inserted at the request of the Arlington Tree Committee

## Description

To see if the Town will vote to amend Title II of the Town Bylaws to establish formal rules and procedures for the Arlington Tree Committee, including but not limited to the number of members, terms of office, and duties and responsibilities; or take any action related thereto.
