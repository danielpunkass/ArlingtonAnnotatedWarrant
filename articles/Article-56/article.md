# Article 56: ZONING BYLAW AMENDMENT / DEVELOPMENT STANDARDS – OFF STREET PARKING AND BICYCLE PARKING

**Sponsor:** Inserted at the request of Neal Mongold and ten registered voters

## Description

To see if the Town will vote to amend Section 5.8.4 F (3) “Development Standards – Off Street Parking and Bicycle Parking” as follows: The bicycle parking requirements and procedures set forth in Section 6.1.12 shall apply in the MDMF and NMF Overlay Districts. Provided however, the Arlington Redevelopment Board may allow the reduction of the long-term bicycle parking requirement to 66% of that requirement in the MBMF and NMF Overlay Districts for properties in which 100% of new units will be affordable; or take any action related thereto.
