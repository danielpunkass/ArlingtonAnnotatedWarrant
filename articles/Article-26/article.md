# Article 26: BYLAW AMENDMENT / RECYCLING REVOLVING FUND

**Sponsor:** Inserted at the request of the Town Manager

## Description

To see if the Town will vote to amend Title I, Article 22 of the Town Bylaws by revising the language of the Recycling Fees Revolving Fund to update the fees, charges or other receipts credited to the fund, and the program or activity expenses payable from the fund; or take any action related thereto.
