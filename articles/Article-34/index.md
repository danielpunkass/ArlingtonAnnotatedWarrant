# Article 34: ACCEPTANCE OF LEGISLATION / WAIVER OF CANINE LICENSING FEE FOR OWNERS 70 AND OLDER

!!! success "Passed on April 27, 2026"

_Inserted at the request of the Town Clerk_

## Description

To see if the Town will vote to accept MGL Chapter 140, Section 139c and allow the waiver of canine licensing fees in Title VIII, Article 2, Section 4F3 for owners 70 years of age or older; or take any action related thereto.
