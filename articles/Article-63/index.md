# Article 63: AMENDMENTS TO FY26 BUDGETS

_Inserted at the request of the Town Manager_

## Description

To see if the Town will vote to appropriate and/or transfer funds between the FY2026 budget and appropriations and the long-term stabilization fund or other available funds as previously voted upon by the 2025 Annual Town Meeting; or take any action related thereto.
