# Article 8: BYLAW AMENDMENT / REGULATION OF OUTDOOR LIGHTING

_Inserted at the request of the Department of Planning and Community Development_

## Description

To see if the Town will vote to amend Title V, Article 14 of the Town Bylaws ("Regulation of Outdoor Lighting") to update the standards for outdoor lighting to comport with the 2024 Dark Skies International model bylaw; or take any action related thereto
