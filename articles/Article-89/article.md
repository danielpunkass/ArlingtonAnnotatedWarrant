# Article 89: RESOLUTION / HYBRID TOWN MEETING

**Sponsor:** Inserted at the request of Alexander Bagnall and ten registered voters

## Description

To see if the Town will vote to support the implementation of hybrid capabilities for Town Meeting; or take any action related thereto.
