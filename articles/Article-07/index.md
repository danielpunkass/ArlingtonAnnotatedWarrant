# Article 7: BYLAW AMENDMENT / ARLINGTON COMMISSION FOR ARTS AND CULTURE ADMINISTRATIVE UPDATES

**Sponsor:** Inserted at the request of the Arlington Commission for Arts and Culture and the Department of Planning and Community Development

## Description

To see if the Town will vote to amend Title II, Article 8 of the Town Bylaws to make administrative updates, including to membership processes and procedures, for the Arlington Commission for Arts and Culture; or take any action related thereto.
