# Article 11: BYLAW AMENDMENT / WETLANDS BYLAW ENFORCEMENT

**Sponsor:** Inserted at the request of the Conservation Commission

## Description

To see if the Town will vote to amend Title IX, Article 2 of the Town Bylaws ("Non-Criminal Disposition of Bylaws") to add the Conservation Agent and Conservation Administrator as enforcing persons of Title V, Article 8 of the Town Bylaws ("Wetlands Protection"); or take any action related thereto.
