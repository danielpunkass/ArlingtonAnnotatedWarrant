# Article 78: APPROPRIATION / HARRY BARBER COMMUNITY SERVICE PROGRAM

**Sponsor:** Inserted at the request of the Council on Aging

## Description

PROGRAM To see if the Town will vote to appropriate a sum of money for the Harry Barber Community Service Program for the Council on Aging, to determine how the money will be raised and expended; or take any action related thereto.
