# Article 80: APPROPRIATION / 51A GROVE STREET BUILDING REPAIRS

**Sponsor:** Inserted at the request of the Town Manager

## Description

To see if the Town will vote to appropriate a sum of money from the Municipal Building Insurance Fund for the cost of emergency repairs to 51A Grove Street; or take any action related thereto.
