# Article 33: ACCEPTANCE OF LEGISLATION / M.G.L. c. 60 SECTION 62A

**Sponsor:** Inserted at the request of Thomas Maloney and ten registered voters

## Description

To see if the Town will vote to adopt the local option provided by General Law Chapter 60 Section 62A, allowing tax collectors to enter into payment agreements for properties in tax title; or take any action related thereto.
