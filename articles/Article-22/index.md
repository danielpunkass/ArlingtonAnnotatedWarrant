# Article 22: BYLAW AMENDMENT / USE OF AREAS UNDER CONTROL OF PARK DEPARTMENT

_Inserted at the request of Elaine Crowder and ten registered voters_

## Description

To see if the Town will vote to amend Title IV, Article 1, which in part protects parks from destructive acts, by expanding protections to park elements not currently listed and clarifying and strengthening consequences for violations; or take any action related thereto.
