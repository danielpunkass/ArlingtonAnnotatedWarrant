**Sponsor:** Inserted at the request of the Town Manager

## Description

To see if the Town will vote to amend Title IX, Article 3 of the Town Bylaws to update existing fee amounts and add any currently omitted services with accompanying appropriate fee structures; or take any action related thereto.
