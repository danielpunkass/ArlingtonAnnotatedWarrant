# Article 42: ZONING BYLAW AMENDMENT / PARKING AND LOADING STANDARDS

**Sponsor:** Inserted at the request of the Redevelopment Board

## Description

To see if the Town will vote to amend Section 6.1.11.C.(3), Parking and Loading Space Standards, of the Zoning Bylaw, to reduce the required minimum width of drive aisles in parking and loa ding areas containing over five spaces; or take any action related thereto.
