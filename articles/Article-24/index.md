# Article 24: BYLAW AMENDMENT / 17 IRVING STREET REVOLVING FUND

_Inserted at the request of the Town Manager_

## Description

To see if the Town will vote to amend Title I, Article 22 of the Town Bylaws by adding a new section to establish and authorize a revolving fund as provided under M.G.L. c. 40 § 3 to utilize monies received through the lease of 17 Irving Street so that those monies may be utilized for the upkeep of the rented facility or space; or take any action related thereto.
