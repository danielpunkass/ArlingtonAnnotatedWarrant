# Article 46: ZONING BYLAW AMENDMENT / ANIMAL DAYCARE USES

!!! success "Passed on May 11, 2026"

_Inserted at the request of the Redevelopment Board_

## Description

To see if the Town will vote to amend Section 2, Definitions, of the Zoning Bylaw, to define animal daycare, and Section 5, District Regulations, of the Zoning Bylaw, to allow animal daycare uses in business districts; or take any action related thereto.
